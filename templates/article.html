Heart Disease Types & Symptoms

Coronary Artery Disease

Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath.
Precautions: Eat healthy, exercise regularly.
Medications: Statins, blood thinners.
Know More

Arrhythmia

Symptoms: Palpitations, dizziness, chest discomfort.
Precautions: Avoid caffeine, manage stress.
Medications: Beta-blockers, antiarrhythmic drugs.
Know More

Heart Failure

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, fatigue.
Precautions: Limit salt intake, avoid alcohol.
Medications: Diuretics, ACE inhibitors.
Know More

Heart Valve Disease

Symptoms: Chest pain, fatigue.
Precautions: Regular check-ups, control blood pressure.
Medications: Blood thinners, ACE inhibitors.
Know More

Cardiomyopathy

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, swelling in legs.
Precautions: Healthy diet, avoid alcohol.
Medications: Beta-blockers, diuretics.
Know More

Pericarditis

Symptoms: Chest pain, fever.
Precautions: Avoid infections, manage stress.
Medications: Pain relievers, antibiotics.
Know More

Aortic Aneurysm

Symptoms: Severe chest or abdomen pain.
Precautions: Regular screenings, control blood pressure.
Medications: Blood pressure meds, cholesterol-lowering drugs.
Know More
